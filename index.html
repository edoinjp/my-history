Half Sword Presentation

Half Sword is Medieval Fencing Simulator, where you can use historiacally correct weapons and armor in physics-based combat.

Being a HEMA (Historical European Martial Arts) enthusiast myself, I decided to create a game, where players could feel like they are participating in real Medieval Combat. There is much more depth and complexity in sword fighting, that it is usually portrayed in popular media. In this game I want to show how a brutal beauty of Knightly Martial Arts. With custom physics system you can feel every swing of your weapon, a weight of your armor and see how each part of your equipment functions on the battlefield.
